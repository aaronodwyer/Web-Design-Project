About Us!

MSKG was established in 2002 by Sensei Christopher 'Git' Knowles who is a 3rd Dan Black Belt. Our class days are Tuesdays U12 6pm-7pm, 12+ 7pm-8pm and Thursdays U12 6pm-7pm, 12+ 7pm-8pm, we teach a mix between fighting for future competitions and kata for belt gradings. When you start off as a white belt and your path will be as follows:

  • White
  • Yellow
  • Orange
  • Green
  • Purple(1)
  • Purple(2)
  • Brown(1)
  • Brown(2)
  • Brown(3)
  • Black Belt (1st Dan - 10th Dan)

After you have trained for a while and if we think you are ready, we will send you on your first competition in Ireland, and if that goes well, you will be selected for an international competition. As a club, we have been all over Europe competing in different competitions and have been lucky with our training and brought home many different medals and trophies.
