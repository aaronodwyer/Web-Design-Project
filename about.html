More Information

The MooreField Shotokan Karate Group club is an affiliated Shotokan Karate club throughout Dublin, Kildare and Meath. The number of clubs continues to grow with a number in the Dublin area and surrounding counties. The club continues to thrive within its local communities welcoming new members in a disciplined but friendly environment in our Sports Centres.

The MSKG celebrated its 10th year in January 2023 and continues to grow and develop Shotokan Karate in Ireland. The MSKG has over 300 students across all its clubs.
